Jaswanth Reddy Katthi

Currently, I'm a Camera systems engineer at Qualcomm India Private Limited. I was a Masters student at Indian Institute of Science, Bengaluru, where my research focused on deploying deep learning models to model the relationship between a natural audio stimulus and the corresponding human brain response at LEAP Lab.


Research Interests: I am very much interested in understanding how humans can perceive, think (also take decisions) and learn memories, and replicating them in machines. Formally, I am interested in Machine Learning, Language Technologies (Speech and Natural Language Processing), Neuroscience, Computer Vision, and Reinforcement Learning.
